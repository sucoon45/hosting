Frequently Asked Questions about SSL

An SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) certificate is a digital certificate that authenticates a website's identity and enables an encrypted connection. It ensures that all data passed between the web server and browsers remain private and integral.

The right SSL depends on your needs. For personal sites, a DV SSL is usually sufficient. Businesses often opt for OV SSL for more trust. EV SSL provides the highest level of trust, ideal for e-commerce and financial sites. If you have multiple subdomains, a Wildcard SSL is cost-effective.

DV SSL certificates are typically issued within minutes. OV and EV certificates require more extensive validation and can take a few business days.

SSL installation can be complex. We offer installation services, and many of our hosting plans include free AutoSSL which automatically installs and renews DV SSL certificates. Please check your specific hosting plan details or contact support.
